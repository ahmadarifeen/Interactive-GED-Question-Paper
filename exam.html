Mathematical Reasoning, Part 1

You may NOT use a calculator in Part 1

1. There are 78 sophomores at a school. Each is required to take at least one year of either chemistry or physics, but they may take both. 15 are enrolled in both chemistry and physics, and 47 are enrolled only in chemistry. How many students are enrolled only in physics?

2. Adding 421 to 343 and then subtracting 252 from the sum results in which value?

3. The perimeter of the hexagon below is 240 centimeters. What is the length of each side?

4. If x = -1, then what is the value of x² + x²?

5. If two sides of a triangle are 6.5 and 8.5 inches long, which of the following CANNOT be the length of the third side?

6. What is the value of | 7 - 2 | - | 2 - 7 | ?

7. The average team score for the first 5 basketball games of the season is 45 points. The scores of the first 5 games are 54, 60, 28, 42, and __________
